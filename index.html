Hello!
I'm Bruno, an Electrical Engineer graduated at IMT (Instituto Mauá de Tecnologia). I have a post-graduate degree in Electrical Power Systems from UniFEI (Universidade Federal de Itajubá) and a master's degree in Artificial Intelligence applied to Electrical Power Systems by UFABC (Universidade Federal do ABC).
All of my side projects involve education in one way or another. Besides teaching Mathematics at Chapel School (an international school located in São Paulo), I produce Calculus content for Curso de Cálculo (free content is posted on this Youtube channel on a regular basis), I work one on one with IB students offering private tutoring lessons and I am currently building a questionbank of IBDP Math HL questions in HLMath.com.
